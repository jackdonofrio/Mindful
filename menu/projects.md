#### Daily Inspiration
[Daily Inspiration](https://github.com/Jack1q/Daily-Inspiration) is a simple Twitter bot that tweets inspirational quotes everyday at 8:37 PM to promote mindfulness and living in the present moment. 

### Take a Hike
[Take a Hike](https://github.com/Jack1q/Take-A-Hike) uses Geocoding API and Haversine's formula to determine the nearest hiking trails in Connecticut and sort them based on distance from current position.
